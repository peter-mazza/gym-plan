CARDIO OPTIONS
Heart Rate Zones
Zone 1 · 95–115 BPM · Warm-up / Day 5 cardio
Zone 2 · 115–135 BPM · Fat burn / aerobic base
Zone 3 · 135–155 BPM · Target for finishers · Endorphin zone
Zone 4 · 155–170 BPM · Occasional use only for now
Incline Ramp Alternatives
🏃 Indoor Track (jog)
Southrac's 1/8-mile track. Easy to hit Zone 3 with a moderate jog. Low impact, no learning curve.
🚴 Stationary Bike
Very back-friendly. Best option if your back is flaring. Reliable Zone 3 at moderate resistance.
🪜 Stair Climber
High endorphin output. Glute-heavy — great after leg days. Hits Zone 3 fast.
🚣 Rowing Machine
Full body, excellent conditioning. Learn form before going hard — poor rowing can stress lower back.

Testosterone production requires dietary fat — don't go very low
Priority Order
1. Hit protein first — if you miss one macro, don't let it be this one
2. Hit total calories — being significantly under slows recovery
3. Fill in carbs — especially pre-workout
4. Let fat flex — you're already fat-heavy by habit
Protein Strategy
Whey protein: 2 scoops/day = ~40–50g with zero meal planning.
1 scoop post-workout · 1 scoop at another meal or in a smoothie.
Drinking Days (3x/week)
~3 drinks ≈ 300–400 kcal. On those days:
Drop 100–150g of carbs, not fat.
Keep protein high regardless.
